Representaciones de números negativos

Complemento de 1
\[\begin{equation*} \color{black}8_{10}=\color{blue}0\color{black}0110_{c1} \end{equation*}\]
\[\begin{equation*} \color{black}-8_{10}=\color{blue}1\color{black}1001_{c1} \end{equation*}\]
Complemento de 2
\[\begin{equation*} \color{black}8_{10}=\color{blue}0\color{black}0110_{c2} \end{equation*}\]
\[\begin{equation*} \color{black}-8_{10}=\color{blue}1\color{black}1001_{c1}+1=11010_{c2} \end{equation*}\]
